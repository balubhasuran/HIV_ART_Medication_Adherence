# Cohort Definition

## Inclusion Criteria
Young adults aged 18–29 years with HIV and ART initiation.

Evidence of HIV infection required:
- HIV ICD diagnosis code, and at least one of the following:
  - Relevant HIV-related laboratory result (e.g., CD4 count)
  - ART medication order or dispensing record
  - At least two HIV-related encounters within 60 days of initial diagnosis

## Exclusion Criteria
- Missing diagnostic or prescription codes
- Incomplete or unlinked pharmacy dispensing records
- Implausible data entries
- Insufficient longitudinal data

## Final Cohort
3,246 patients
